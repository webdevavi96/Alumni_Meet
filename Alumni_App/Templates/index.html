Alumni Meet

The Alumni Meet Web App is a dedicated platform designed to connect current students, faculty, and alumni of Mahamaya IT Polytechnic Maharajganj. This web app offers a seamless digital space where users can create profiles based on their role—student, teacher, or alumnus. Alumni can post job opportunities, host virtual meets, and mentor students, while teachers can manage events and foster academic discussions. Students benefit by joining meetings, reading blog posts, and building networks with past graduates. Developed with Django and a Bootstrap-based UI, the app ensures responsive design and a secure, role-based experience to strengthen the lifelong bond between Mahamaya IT Polytechnic and its extended family.
